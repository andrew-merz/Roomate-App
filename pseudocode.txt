MVP pseudocode

//Start Page

User will be prompted to create a new account
    -they will create a username, password, email
        -once account is created user will be prompted to create a new room or join an existing room
        -new room
            -create a room name and unique password
        -existing room
            -enter room password to join the room
    -if they already have an account they will click log in

Once the room has been entered by all of the roomates the status will be displayed
    -users can only have one status at a time but can edit it whenever they feel like
    -the newest status will go to the top of the page and be time stamped
    -users cannot delete their status, only update it 
    -users can comment once on eachothers status'
